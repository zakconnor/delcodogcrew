Who We Are

We're the Crew Your Dog Has Been Dreaming About

Declo Dog Crew was born out of pure, unapologetic love for dogs (and let's be honest, the occasional treat bribe). We're a local team of certified dog walkers serving Delaware County, PA — because your pup deserves someone who gets genuinely excited about their tail wags.

Every walk is an adventure. Every dog is a VIP. And yes, we absolutely do the goofy voice when we talk to them.
