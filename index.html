Who We Are

A dog-walking crew that actually cares

Declo Dog Crew was started by dog owners who were tired of the alternative: unreliable walkers, zero communication, and the sinking feeling of not knowing if your pup was okay. We set out to do it differently.

We're a small, tight-knit team of background-checked, pet first aid certified walkers who treat every dog like it's our own. That means consistent walkers your dog recognizes, real-time updates, and someone who notices when something seems off.

What We Offer

Services built around your dog's needs

Whether your dog needs a daily midday walk or just a quick check-in, we've got a service that fits.

Solo Walk

30 or 60 min

Your dog gets our walker's undivided attention. Ideal for dogs who prefer one-on-one time, are in training, or need a bit more space around other dogs. No pack, no distractions — just a great walk.

Small Group Walk

Max 4 dogs

A small, carefully matched group of dogs out for an adventure together. Great for social dogs who get a little extra energy from having a crew. Groups are capped at four to keep things safe and manageable.

Drop-In Visit

20 min

A midday check-in with feeding, a bathroom break, fresh water, and plenty of attention. Perfect for puppies, senior dogs, or any day your dog just needs someone to pop by.

Puppy Package

Multiple daily

New puppy? We get it — they need a lot. This package includes multiple visits throughout the day to cover frequent bathroom breaks, feeding schedules, and basic routine building.
